## RedLine-Dynasty
A systemic simulation of high-entropy dynastic survival.

## The Simulation
RedLine Dynasty is an OCaml-based simulation engine designed to model the intersection of ancestral lineage, environmental entropy, and an endlessly shifting world governed by inconsistent physics.

In this world, the fundamental laws of nature:Wind, Thunder, Water, and Fire are "hard-coded" into the geography as unstable natural forces. Your objective is to guide a lineage through a fracturing world of familiar and yet different faces. As the Architect, you must navigate the Hierarchy of Stability while managing the dangers of sentient fauna, information security, and the catastrophic risk or reward of forcing a "Redline" breakthrough.

## The Architect's Vision
This project is an exploration of emergent complexity. Unlike traditional strategy games, the world is not a static map,it is a live "Kernel" that reacts to the player's presence. Every technique documented, every sentient beast harvested, and every structure built alters the world's underlying physics.

## Core Systems
# The Ancestral Pipeline: 
  A persistent, immutable data structure tracking the evolutionary history, technical debt, and bloodline traits of your lineage.
# Kernel-Merge Logic:
A functional engine that governs how disparate elemental biomes hybridize when forced together, creating emergent "Storm" or "Void-Fire" zones.
# The Commodity-Citizen Paradox: 
Sentient fauna are not mere mobs; they are political agents capable of learning your clan’s techniques. You must decide whether to treat them as citizens to be traded with or as high-tier resource nodes to be harvested.
# Hierarchy of Stability: 
A transition from fragile diplomatic bonds to operational mitigation and finally to technological supremacy or all of them at once.
